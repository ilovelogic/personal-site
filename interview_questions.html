A step inside my mind...

Three questions rarely asked in interviews that reveal important things

Question 1: How do you define a friend?

Answer 1: A friend is a person who wills your good and for whom you will the good.

Question 2: What do you find most beautiful about the person you love the most?

Answer 2: Self-sacrifical love taken beyond the ends of the Earth

Question 3: What do you want to be remembered for?

Answer 3: I actually don't want to be remembered for anything. I just hope that my past actions will ripple toward the good after my death.

A. Kelley · School Email · CSE 134B, Summer 2026
