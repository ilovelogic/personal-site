Creating a good environment

"Have nothing in your home that you do not know to be useful or believe to be beautiful."
- William Morris
I live by this. Everything in my room is there for a reason, whether it is reminders that I'm striving to reach spiritual union with Christ or a piano positioned so that I can look out of the window while playing.

Aligning all the sets

Your ability to learn well depends on the health of your heartset, soulset, mindset, and bodyset.

Heartset

"What you are in love with, what seizes your imagination, will affect everything. It will decide what will get you out of bed in the morning, what you do with your evenings, how you spend your weekends, what you read, whom you know, what breaks your heart, and what amazes you with joy and gratitude.
Fall in Love, stay in love, and it will decide everything."
- Fr. Pedro Arrupe

Soulset

"The best course to prevent falling into the pit is to keep at the greatest distance."
- Thomas Watson
I use my journal to track how I respond to temptations and what does and does not work.

Mindset

Change the way you look at things, and the things you look at change."
- Dr. Wayne Dyer
I am always asking myself why I am doing what I am doing. If the reason is beautiful, good, and true, than nothing of this Earth can deter me from my pursuit. If I see that my reason is, at its root, meaningless, I can let go of that chase to focus on what matters.

Bodyset

"Take care of your body. It's the only place you have to live."
- Jim Rohn
We must tend to the garden soil if we want the flowers growing from it to bloom. Our bodies are our means of interacting in this world, and all the ways we can make a difference are, accordingly, dependent on our physical health. One does not put crude oil into a Mustang nor does one let a priceless violin degrade due to lack of use. Similarly, we ought treat our bodies with the same care and respect we would give to these treasures, these possessions worth infintely less than a human being.
